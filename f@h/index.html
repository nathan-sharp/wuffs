How To Start Folding

  1. Download Folding at Home from the official site.

  4. Choose your power level and let your machine fold.

Why Fold?

  • Support open scientific research on major diseases.
  • Use hardware that would otherwise sit idle.
  • Compete and collaborate as a fun community team.

Every work unit helps. Whether you fold 24/7 or just when AFK, you are making a difference.
